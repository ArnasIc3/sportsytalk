Our story

How was SporstyTalk founded?

In the bustling halls of Vilnius University, a group of first-year students found themselves united by their shared love for sports. Arnas Martinkus, Skirmantas Strašinskas, Mantas Globys, Adomas Jasiukevičius, Kasparas Šumskis, and Justas Aleknavičius were brought together not just by their academic pursuits but by a common passion for staying updated on the latest sports news. As they navigated through their first year, attending classes and bonding over sports events, the group noticed a gap in the way sports news was being delivered. They longed for a platform that not only provided up-to-the-minute updates but also infused a sense of excitement and entertainment into the news delivery process.Fuelled by their entrepreneurial spirit, the six students decided to embark on a project that would not only fulfill their craving for dynamic sports news but also cater to the broader student community. And so, "SportsyTalk" was conceived—an innovative sports news website designed to deliver the freshest, most useful, and entertaining news in the world of sports.

What we plan to do in the future:

  • 1. Integrate user feedback functionality.
  • 2. Diversify the content with content like video highlights, interviews and feature articles.
  • 3. Enhance the user experience.
  • 4. Strengthen the website's presence on various social media platforms to expand the community.
